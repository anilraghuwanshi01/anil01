Getting your picture taken

I’ll have to admit, having a camera on your face is not the most comfortable thing in the world, I get it. I approach photography a little differently than most. The MOST IMPORTANT THING IS MAKING YOU FEEL COMFORTABLE AS POSSIBLE. I don't won’t to feel you uncomfortable and make you do anything that feels FORCED OR FAKE (That’s just weird). I will give some direction like where to put your hands, but for the most part, my job will be to DOCUMENTARY your unique love story and the particular moment in time.
